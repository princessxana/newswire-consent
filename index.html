NewsWire Alerts Consent

This page documents consent for SMS alerts sent by PRINCESS XANA Alerts.

The recipient is the owner/operator of this private monitoring tool and has explicitly opted in to receive SMS alerts related to news and events.

Message frequency varies based on triggered events. Message and data rates may apply.

The recipient can opt out at any time by disabling alerts in the application or replying STOP. For help, reply HELP.

This messaging is for news and events alerting only and is not used for marketing.
