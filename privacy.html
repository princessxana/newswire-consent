Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

NewsWire Alerts is a private monitoring and notification system used to send event-driven SMS alerts to the opted-in user.

Information Collected

NewsWire Alerts may collect and store the user's name, email address, and mobile phone number when provided through the opt-in workflow.

How Information Is Used

This information is used only to deliver requested SMS alerts related to configured market, news, geopolitical, keyword, or ticker monitoring events.

Sharing of Information

Mobile information will not be sold or shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. Information may be shared only with service providers necessary to deliver requested messages, such as Twilio.

Opt-Out

Users may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message. Users may request help by replying HELP.

Contact

Questions about this policy may be directed through the contact method provided by the operator of NewsWire Alerts.
